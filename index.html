About

Georgette Bleau

Stories with heart, stakes, and unforgettable twists.

Georgette Bleau is a storyteller with a passion for creating bold, high‑stakes worlds where human emotion collides with extraordinary circumstances. Her writing blends sharp tension, layered characters, and emotional depth to pull readers into page‑turning narratives. Based in Texas with East Coast roots, Georgette draws inspiration from her life in real estate, family legacy, and the resilience of the human spirit.

She is currently developing multiple projects, including Mercy, a near‑future thriller where the public votes to exile prisoners to an island with no escape; On the Hill, a powerful family drama inspired by her wife’s family of seven sisters who grew up on their own land; and The Therapist, a thriller with a sci‑fi twist about grief, consequence, and the price of a wish.

Follow along on Instagram @bleauwrites.

Mercy Thriller / Dystopian • Series

In a new world where justice is decided by public vote, prisoners are exiled to an isolated island with no escape. When a wrongfully convicted man is sent there, he must navigate deadly alliances, ruthless enemies, and the dark truth behind the system, fighting to survive long enough to prove his innocence.

On the Hill Family Drama • Series

Seven sisters and their children are determined to stay together on the land they grew up on after their mother’s death. But as they fight to protect their legacy, buried secrets and old wounds surface, threatening to tear them apart and rewrite what “family” means.

Inspired by my wife’s family history.

The Therapist Thriller / Sci‑Fi • Feature

A grieving father is offered a chance to bring his daughter back, but every miracle has a price. As reality frays and consequences mount, he must decide how far he’ll go to hold on to the life he lost.
